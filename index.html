Who We Are

NexBoard started as a small side-project shelf for leftover ESP32 boards and grew into a proper store for hobbyists and students who don't want to wait weeks for parts to arrive from overseas marketplaces. Everything we sell is tested before it ships, so you spend your time building instead of debugging a dead board.

We stock development boards, wireless modules, sensors, and the small accessories that always seem to go missing right when a project deadline is closest — jumper wires, breadboards, and voltage regulators included.

Most orders within Dhaka go out the same day, and we're always happy to answer questions about wiring or code over email if you get stuck partway through a build.
